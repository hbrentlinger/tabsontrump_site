CAMPAIGN PROMISES AND THEIR CURRENT STATUS

The people who gave Donald Trump their vote during the 2016 election did so because the ideas that he proposed along the way were aligned with their own political views. Suprisingly, we still have yet to see him take any action whatsover on many of the things that he promised to do as president of the United States. The following is the current status of many of the promised that Trump made along the campaign trail.
